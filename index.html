About Me

I am a teaching assistant professor and researcher in computer architecture in the ECE department at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. I am currently leading efforts to develop hardware frameworks for machine learning in computer architecture, and a new open-source hardware foundation at UIUC. My research is supported by industry sponsors: Samsung, AMD, and Google. I am also currently involved in the development of new courses in VLSI design and hardware verification.
