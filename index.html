About Me

I am a teaching assistant professor at the ECE department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (Institution Homepage). I am currently involved in the development of new courses in VLSI design, hardware verification, and AI/ML for systems and architecture. My research is primarily in the field of computer architecture and is partially funded by Google Research.

Research Interests

Computer Architecture: CPU and accelerator microarchitectures, methods and designs to generalize acceleration.

AI/ML for Systems and Architecture: ML for microarchitecture search, VLSI implementation, and systems optimization.

Models of Computing: Non-conventional models of computing, emerging technologies.
